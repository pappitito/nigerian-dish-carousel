Nkwobi

Nkwobi is a Nigerian dish made from spiced cow foot. It's served in posh restaurants and beer bars in Nigeria. Nigerians love it. The only problem is that cowfeet take a long time to boil. Slow cookers or pressure cookers are good since they cook food more slowly. If neither option was available, I found that boiling and freezing cowfeet was a great substitute. My freezer always has ready-to-eat meats. This reduces my kitchen time.
